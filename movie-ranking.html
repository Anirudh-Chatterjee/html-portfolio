The Best Movies according to Anirudh

My top 3 movies of all time

1. Fast Five

The fifth film in the fast and furious franchise. Absolutely loved it. Everything was perfect.
The direction.
The music.
The action.
All of it.

2. Sholay

Bollywood classic and a timeless one at that.

3. Rush Hour 3

Comedic gold. With Jackie Chan and Chris Tucker at the lead, this film was jam-packed with action and funny moments.
